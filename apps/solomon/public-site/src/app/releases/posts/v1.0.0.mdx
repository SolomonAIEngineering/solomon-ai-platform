### May 15th Release

Solomon AI equips your practice with a platform designed to conduct thorough financial stress tests.

### Features

- **Financial Stress Test**: Analyze your practice's financial health and identify areas for improvement based on a multitude of scenarios.
- **Expense Management**: Track and manage expenses to optimize your practice's financial performance.
